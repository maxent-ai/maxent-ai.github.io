Identity Services Process
At Maxent, we believe in the transformative power of well-executed products. Our process is driven by collaboration, tailored to the unique challenges of each project. Here's how we work:
Discover: Our journey starts with a call to delve into your business problem. Depending on complexity, we may follow up with clarifications via email, a second technical call, or a customized offer.
Build: Inspired by Agile workflows and Design Thinking, our building phase involves:
  1. Problem exploration: Understanding the core problem and identifying sub-problems for a holistic view.
  2. Unlocking opportunities: Ranking problems by impact, formulating hypotheses, and lean prototyping.
  3. Lean prototyping: Transforming hypotheses into tangible prototypes, refining them iteratively based on feedback.
  4. Solution validation: Compiling findings, analyzing data to validate hypotheses, and guiding strategic decision-making.
  5. Evolutionary implementation: Developing tailored solutions aligned with validated insights, iterating to adapt to evolving needs.
Integrate: We ensure seamless integration into your ecosystems with checkpoints. Our commitment extends to post-implementation support, empowering you to take ownership. Comprehensive training programs and documentation facilitate self-sufficiency for long-term success.
